# Visual Mockup Reference Guide

Based on the provided mockup, here's what the design should look and feel like:

## Overall Aesthetic

**Inspiration**: Hand-drawn travel journal meets modern cartography app
- Stained-glass logo illustration (lighthouse, church, hills, bike wheel)
- Warm pastel palette (beige/cream backgrounds)
- Visible borders and frames (black outlines)
- Mix of landscape and portrait orientations
- Documentary photography style

## Color Applications

### Charbon (#272624)
- All text
- All borders
- Principle card background (white text)

### Creme (#FAEED4)
- Page background
- Card backgrounds
- Map interior

### Beige (#DACEBB)
- Principle card background
- Placeholder backgrounds
- Secondary panels

### Rouge (#F3492F)
- Section title borders (left accent)
- Route line on map
- Energy, movement

### Vert (#4D9678)
- Encounter card borders
- Some horizon card borders
- Nature, growth

### Jaune (#FEAF2C)
- Principle card background
- Warmth, optimism
- Subtle accents

### Bleu (#216173)
- Some horizon card borders
- Map frame
- Water, depth, calm

## Photography Direction

### DO:
- Natural light preferred
- Slightly desaturated
- Documentary/reportage style
- Show weather, wear, authenticity
- Empty landscapes
- Candid people moments
- Film photography aesthetic

### DON'T:
- Over-saturated colors
- Instagram filters
- Posed portraits
- Professional cycling gear glamour shots
- Perfect composition
- HDR effects
- Stock photo smiles

## Typography Style

### Headings
- Bold, uppercase for some (Principle cards)
- Mixed case for observational titles
- Clean, sans-serif
- High contrast

### Body Copy
- Readable size (16px base)
- Line height 1.6
- Short sentences
- Observational, not persuasive
- Present tense preferred

## Layout Philosophy

### NOT perfect grids
- Vary column widths (1fr, 1.2fr, 0.9fr)
- Slight irregularities welcomed
- Borders not always aligned

### Breathing room
- Generous padding (2-4rem)
- Cards have internal space
- Not cramped

### Visible structure
- Borders define zones
- Frames contain content
- Section dividers clear
- No floating elements
